Bachelor of Social Work

The Bachelor of Social Work (BSW) is a professionally accredited undergraduate program designed to prepare students for a rewarding career in social work and community service. This program provides a strong foundation in social work theory, ethics, human behavior, and practical skills through classroom learning and hands-on fieldwork. Students gain the knowledge and experience needed to support individuals, families, and communities in diverse settings such as schools, hospitals, non-profit organizations, and government agencies. Graduates are well-equipped for entry-level social work roles or for pursuing advanced studies in the field.
